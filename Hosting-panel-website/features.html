Core Features

Discover the technology and support that powers your gaming experience.

Extreme Performance Hardware

We don't compromise on performance. Our entire infrastructure is built on the latest generation of hardware. We utilize high-clock speed AMD Ryzen and Intel Core i9 CPUs paired with enterprise-grade NVMe SSDs. This means faster map loading, smoother gameplay, and a lag-free experience even for the most demanding games and mods.

Comprehensive DDoS Protection

Your server is always online, guaranteed. We provide robust, always-on DDoS protection that can mitigate complex attacks of any size. Our global network filters malicious traffic before it ever reaches your server, ensuring uninterrupted gameplay for you and your players.

Intuitive Control Panel

Manage your server with ease using our custom-built control panel. We've designed it from the ground up to be powerful for experts, yet simple for beginners. Enjoy one-click mod installers, full FTP access, automated backups, and real-time performance metrics all in one place.

24/7 Expert Support

Ran into an issue? Our team of gaming enthusiasts and support experts is available around the clock via live chat and tickets. We know the games we host inside and out, so we can help you with everything from initial setup to complex mod configurations.
