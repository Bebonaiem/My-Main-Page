Refund Policy

Read our policy regarding refunds for purchases made on Bebo Hosting.

1. Refund Eligibility

We understand that sometimes purchases don't meet expectations. Refunds are available on a case-by-case basis within 14 days of your purchase. To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Contact our support team within 14 days of purchase
  • Provide valid order details and transaction information
  • Explain the reason for your refund request
  • Items must not have been delivered or used in-game

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items and circumstances are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Items that have already been delivered and used in-game
  • Purchases made more than 14 days ago
  • Items purchased during special promotions or sales (unless otherwise stated)
  • Digital items that have been activated or redeemed
  • Purchases made with promotional codes or discounts
  • Items purchased for accounts that have been banned for violations

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our support team through our contact form
  • Include your order number or transaction ID
  • Provide your Minecraft username and server information
  • Explain the reason for your refund request in detail
  • Include any relevant screenshots or evidence if applicable

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 3 business days and process approved refunds within 5-10 business days.

4. Refund Processing

Once your refund is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method
  • Processing times vary by payment provider (3-10 business days)
  • You will receive a confirmation email when the refund is processed
  • Any items associated with the refund will be removed from your account

5. Chargebacks and Disputes

We strongly encourage you to contact our support team before initiating a chargeback or payment dispute. Initiating a chargeback without contacting our support team first may result in:

  • Permanent ban from Bebo Hosting and affiliated servers
  • Loss of access to all purchased items
  • Inability to make future purchases
  • Additional fees and processing costs

6. Special Circumstances

We may consider refunds in special circumstances such as:

  • Technical issues preventing item delivery
  • Server maintenance or downtime affecting gameplay
  • Duplicate purchases due to system errors
  • Items purchased for the wrong account or server

7. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds for:

  • Bundles where only some items are affected
  • Time-limited items that were unavailable for a significant period
  • Items that were partially delivered or malfunctioning

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We will notify users of significant changes via email or website notification.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about our refund policy or need to request a refund, please contact our support team. We're here to help resolve any issues you may have with your purchase.
