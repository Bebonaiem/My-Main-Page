Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my purchased items?

All items are delivered instantly after purchase. Simply log in to the server and your items will be waiting for you. If you don't receive them immediately, try relogging or contact our support team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and various cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Can I get a refund for my purchase?

We offer refunds on a case-by-case basis. If you're unsatisfied with your purchase or didn't receive your items, please contact our support team within 14 days of purchase.

How do I become a server partner?

We're always looking to partner with quality Minecraft servers. Please send us a partnership inquiry through our contact form and we'll get back to you with more information.
