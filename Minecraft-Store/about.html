About Partner Hosting

Learn more about our Minecraft server store and the team behind it

Our Story

Partner Hosting was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate Minecraft players who wanted to create a better way for server owners to monetize their servers and for players to purchase in-game items.

What started as a small project has grown into one of the most trusted Minecraft server stores, serving thousands of players across multiple servers worldwide.

Our mission is to provide a seamless, secure, and enjoyable shopping experience for Minecraft players while supporting server owners in maintaining and improving their communities.
